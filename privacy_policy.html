1. Introduction

Welcome to GymTracker ("we," "our," "us," or the "App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use GymTracker on iOS devices.

By using GymTracker, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Health & Fitness Data

GymTracker collects and stores the following fitness-related data locally on your device:

  • Workout Records: Exercise names, sets, reps, weight, duration, dates
  • Body Measurements: Weight, height, body fat percentage, muscle mass, and other measurements you manually enter
  • Workout Templates: Custom workout plans you create
  • Custom Exercises: Exercises you add to the app
  • Progress Data: Historical trends and statistics calculated from your workouts
⚠️ Important: All health and fitness data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do NOT have access to this data on our servers.

2.2 iCloud Sync Data

If you enable iCloud sync:

  • Workout data is synced across your Apple devices using Apple's CloudKit
  • iCloud User Identifier: A unique, anonymous identifier provided by Apple to manage sync (we cannot identify you personally from this ID)
  • All synced data is encrypted by Apple and stored in your iCloud account, not on our servers

2.3 Purchase Data

If you purchase GymTracker Premium:

  • Purchase Status: Whether you have an active Premium subscription
  • Transaction ID: Provided by Apple's StoreKit to verify your purchase
  • All payment processing is handled by Apple - we never see your payment information (credit card, billing address, etc.)

2.4 Device Information

The App may access basic device information for functionality purposes:

  • Device Model & iOS Version: To ensure compatibility and optimize performance
  • App Version: To provide support and troubleshooting
  • Language Preference: To display the app in your preferred language

2.5 Data We DO NOT Collect

GymTracker does NOT collect:

  • ❌ Your name, email address, or phone number
  • ❌ Location data or GPS coordinates
  • ❌ Contacts or address book
  • ❌ Photos or camera access (except when YOU choose to take progress photos, which stay local)
  • ❌ Browsing history or activity in other apps
  • ❌ Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • ❌ Third-party tracking or analytics data

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your workout data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is protected by your device's encryption and security features (Face ID, Touch ID, passcode).

4.2 iCloud Storage

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service. Apple encrypts this data both in transit and at rest. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Important: iCloud sync is optional. You can disable it in the App settings.

4.3 Security Measures

  • ✅ Data is encrypted on your device and in iCloud
  • ✅ We use Apple's secure APIs (SwiftData, CloudKit, StoreKit)
  • ✅ No data is transmitted to third-party servers
  • ✅ App updates are distributed securely through the App Store
⚠️ Your Responsibility: Keep your device secure with a strong passcode. Enable two-factor authentication for your Apple ID. We are not liable for unauthorized access due to lost or stolen devices.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

6.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete the app or manually delete workouts
  • iCloud Data: Stored in your iCloud account until you disable iCloud sync or delete the data
  • Purchase Data: Stored by Apple indefinitely to verify your purchase

6.2 How to Delete Your Data

Delete Local Data:

  1. Open GymTracker
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete All Data
  3. Confirm deletion (this is irreversible!)

Delete iCloud Data:

  1. iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage
  2. Find GymTracker
  3. Tap Delete Data

Delete the App:

  1. Long-press the GymTracker icon on your home screen
  2. Tap Remove App → Delete App
  3. This deletes local data but NOT iCloud data (delete separately if needed)
